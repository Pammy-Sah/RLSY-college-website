Ram Lakhan Singh Yadav College, Bettiah (West Champaran) is a constituent unit of B.R.A. Bihar University, Muzaffarpur. This college runs by highly qualified teachers from undergraduate to the Graduate level. In the light of vocational spectrum B.C.A. , Industrial chemistry, Fish and Fisheries, CND and mass communication are also being started teaching in our premises this college exist to provide a liberal education for young people who will be called to intellectual, moral and professional leadership and to fosher in them a lifelong commitment to the quest for truth. In addition to the teaching and learning this college initiate NCC, NSS, Cultural Programmes and sports activities like cricket, Hockey, Basketball, Volleyball, Badminton in separate courts. Our students participate in Inter University tournaments, State Level and national level games and R.D.C. Parades. Students and guardians played a vital role in the continuous development of this college.

We believe that this institution will be counted among the premier institutions of the state and centre due to the positive cooperation of the people of west champaran.
