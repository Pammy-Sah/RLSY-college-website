History of BCA

Ram Lakhan Si ngh Yadav college, Bettiah was founded in 1975 nearest to the Bus Stand and Railway station Bettiah. It was established in the remote area of West Champaran where literacy is very poor in the comparison of other districts of Bihar. This college is sprawal in 36.21 acres of land covering with green plants. The students of this college come from poor stata.

This college is a constituent unit of B.R.A. Bihar University Muzaffarpur and NAAC accreditated Grade “B”. Our aim is to promote not only higher education but the intellectual disciplines and the search of personal value that enables its graduate throughout their lives. In the light of these aims the college has developed a diversified academic programs suet as Arts, Science, Commerce and vocational courses suet as B.C.A., CND, Industrial chemistry, Fish and Fisheries and Mass Communication and Journalism. Our high priority is placed on quality teaching and on developing a community of learning among its faculty students, teachers and administrative this college is continuously run by efficient administrators, learned teachers and trained Non-Teaching staff to enhance higher education.
